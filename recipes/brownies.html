Brownies

Brownie recipe created by Orlando Murrin at BBC Good Food. "A foolproof brownie recipe for a squidgy chocolate bake."

The recipe takes about 60 minutes in total for prep and cooking and can be cut into 16 squares. Follow the 16 easy steps below for the perfect brownies.

Ingredients

  • 185g unsalted butter
  • 185g dark chocolate
  • 85g plain flour
  • 40g cocoa powder
  • 50g white chocolate
  • 50g milk chocolate
  • 3 large eggs
  • 275g golden caster sugar
