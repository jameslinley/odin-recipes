Speedy lentil coconut curry recipe

Description

Quick and easy curry with great depth of flavour - it tastes like it's been cooked for hours. It's healthy, low in fat and calories. Original recipe link

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, roughly chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, roughly chopped
  • 1 carrot, roughly chopped
  • 1 red or green chilli, roughly chopped
  • 10g piece of ginger, peeled and chopped
  • 1 tsp vegetable oil
  • 1 1/2 tbsp tikka masala curry paste
  • 400g can cooked green lentils
  • 220ml light coconut milk
  • 200g frozen peas
  • 10g coriander, roughly chopped
  • 200g cooked brown rice
  • 4 tbsp light coconut or natural yoghurt, to serve

Steps

  1. Add onion, garlic, chilli, carrot ands ginger in a food processor and blitz to a smooth paste.
  2. Heat oil in a medium saucepan over a medium heat and cook the veg paste for 4-5 mins until fragrant and soft.
  3. Add curry paste and cook for 1 min, then add lentils and stir to combine.
  4. Pour in coconut milk and add 150ml water, bring to boil.
  5. Reduce heat and simmer for 10 mins until thick and creamy.
  6. Add pease for 5 mnins, season well.
  7. Stir in most coriander, divide curry between four bowls with rice.
  8. Sprinkle remaining coriander on top, serve with yoghurt.
