Speedy sausage stroganoff tagliatelle

Description

Budget friendly quick sausage stroganoff tagliatelle for a simple family dinner. This filling bowl takes just five minutes to prepare. Original recipe link

Ingredients

  • 20g unsalted butter
  • olive oil
  • 6 pork sausages
  • 350g chestnut mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 tsp sweek smoked paprika
  • 300ml soured cream
  • 1/2 tbsp wholegrain mustard
  • 150ml beef stock
  • 400g dried tagliatelle
  • small bunch parlsey, chopped

Steps

  1. Heat butter and oil in frying pan over medium-high heat until foaming.
  2. Squeeze large chunks of sausage meat out of skins into pan, cook for 5-8 mins until golden brown.
  3. Add mushrooms and cook for further 5 minutes until starting to turn brown.
  4. Stir in paprika and cook for 1 min before stirring in soured cream, mustard and stock.
  5. Bring to summer and season to taste.
  6. Cook pasta in a large pan of salted water, add to the sauce and half of parsley.
  7. Serve in deep bowls and sprinkle remaining parsley on top.
